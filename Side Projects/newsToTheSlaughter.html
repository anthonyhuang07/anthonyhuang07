Patrick Maloney Killed - Police Investigating

Patrick Maloney, former policeman, dies of an unknown cause.

Rick Astley · Written: July 12, 1955 | Morty Astley · Posted: June 6, 2009
Patrick Maloney's House, 2009

On July 11, 1955, Patrick Maloney, former policeman, was killed. Police say that this might be a murder, as they found a wound on his head. They say it might be from a hard object, like a club.

"Patrick Maloney had been killed by a blow on the back of the head administered with a heavy blunt instrument, almost certainly a large piece of metal. They were looking for the weapon. The murderer may have taken it with him, but on the other hand he may have thrown it away or hidden it somewhere on the premises," said Jack Noonan, one of the policemen investigating.

The woman who reported Patrick's death was Mary Maloney, his wife. We do know however, that it definitely isn't her who killed Patrick. First, a woman does not have enough strength to kill a policeman. She was also at the grocer during Patrick's murder, and her emotions for Patrick were completely real.

Later that night, the policemen were seen eating a leg of lamb. We don't know why, but we're assuming that they were just hungry from all that hard work searching for the murderer.

The same type of lamb that the policemen were seen eating.

It's been a day since the murder, and the search goes on. The police will be searching until they find the murderer. But for now, there's a murderer on the loose. Stay on guard.
